Om Beach, Gokarna

Om is a natural port in the Gokarna District in Karnataka, India. Located six kilometers to the west of Gokarna, it is an important port and a major fishing harbor on the Karnataka coast. The town of Gokarnaa is associated largely with settlements of the Mogaveera fisherman community.
